Meet the Team Behind the Elegance

Strong Tower Hall is guided by a team of passionate professionals who bring expertise, warmth, and a relentless commitment to excellence to everything they do.

Chief Executive Officer

Mrs. Abiola Ayeni

A Barrister-at-Law by training and a visionary entrepreneur at heart, Mrs. Ayeni founded Strong Tower Hall driven by a deep passion for hospitality and people.

Her distinguished legal background informs her meticulous, detail-oriented approach to business, while her experience as a Fashion Designer gives her an innate sense of style and aesthetics.

Over the years, she has successfully delivered high-profile events for some of Lagos’s most discerning clientele.

A devoted wife and mother, she leads Strong Tower Hall with grace, purpose, and an unwavering commitment to quality.

Hall Manager

Mrs. Mosun Oduyemi

A Business Administration graduate with a sharp eye for operations and a genuine love for the events industry, Mrs. Oduyemi is the engine that keeps Strong Tower Hall running smoothly.

She oversees all hall bookings, coordinates compliance and financial obligations, and ensures every client’s experience is seamless from first enquiry to final farewell.

Always current with the latest trends in event management and hospitality, she brings energy, professionalism, and a warm personal touch to every interaction.

She is a devoted wife and mother.
