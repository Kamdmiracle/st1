Our Story

Heritage & Community

A Statement of Quality

Strong Tower Hall was founded with a single, unwavering purpose: to give the Ikoyi community and beyond a venue worthy of their most important celebrations.

Strategically located directly opposite the Ikoyi Registry, we are a natural choice for couples beginning their forever after and professionals hosting events that demand distinction.

We are more than a hall — we are a statement of quality, care, and community.

Looking Forward

Our Vision

To be the number one venue of choice in Lagos — a space that sets the gold standard in hospitality, blending Nigerian cultural richness with world-class standards.

We aspire to play a meaningful role in the growth of Nigeria’s tourism and hospitality sector.

Our Purpose

Our Mission

To deliver a venue experience that is breathtaking for hosts, comfortable and welcoming for guests, and fully equipped for vendors.

We exist to make every event feel effortless — and extraordinary.
